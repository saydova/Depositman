Requirements for Opening a Bank Account

1. ID Card: Provide a government-issued photo ID (e.g., National ID card, passport).
2. Tax Identification Number (TIN): If applicable, especially for business accounts.
3. Address Proof: Submit a recent utility bill, bank statement, or other official documents showing your current address.

Requirements for Making a Deposit

1. Minimum Deposit: Check if there is a minimum deposit amount required, especially for opening an account.
2. Maximum Deposit: Be aware of any maximum deposit limits, particularly for cash deposits.
3. Receipt: Always request or keep a receipt or confirmation for cash or check deposits.
4. Online Confirmation: For electronic deposits, ensure you receive an email or notification confirming the transaction.
